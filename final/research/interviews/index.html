Target Audience Interviews

Laura Yien

Collectively, Rheiana and I interviewed 4 people. Edyn, Jenna, and Claire are current 1st-year students at UC Davis. Franki is a freshman at DePaul University in Chicago but spent a few summers in Davis visiting with family. We chose to interview first-year UC Davis students because they are new to Davis and would potentially be interested in our scavenger hunt app that aims to encourage people to explore and learn about downtown Davis. We interviewed Franki because they are unfamiliar with the Davis area and we wanted to get a sense of what activities they would be interested in to learn more.

Through our interviews, we learned that the UC Davis orientation does not really have a component that encourages downtown Davis exploration. Students who went downtown, did because their orientation leaders decided to take their group on their own. We also learned that most students do not know much about the history of Davis and are interested in learning more. Prior to coming here, most students only knew about the cows, eggheads, and that UC Davis is an agriculture focused school. From Franki's interview, we also learned that the surrounding community plays a big role in the college decision making process, so it is important for prospective students to get a sense of the entire city not just UC Davis.

From these interviews, we have gathered that incoming students have very limited knowledge about the town of Davis and would be interested in learning more. Additionally, an interactive scavenger hunt would introduce them to Davis places and help them acclimate to their new environment. These findings have provided evidence for the value of our scavenger hunt app and will help inform our decisions as we finalize our concept.
